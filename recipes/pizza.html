Pizza Recipe

Description

This pizza recipe is great for a delicious, thin, crispy wood fired pizza. Neopolitan pizzas are thin, crispy and chewy and will please anyone you serve it to.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Meat (optional)

Steps

  1. Knead dought in to a pizza shape
  2. Put sauce on dough
  3. Put cheese on sauce
  4. Put pizza in oven
  5. remove pizza from oven when cooked
